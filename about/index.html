# Hello, Welcome to New Elite School, located in the heart of Katy, TX. We hope you have a wonderful learning journey!

Why does Ms. Joy start New Elite School?

As a working mom of two children, Ms. Joy understands the importance of finding good learning centers for kids. She believes that good quality classes, healthy food, and a joyful environment benefit both kids and parents.

About Ms. Joy:

Ms. Joy earned her master's degree in Applied Economics in the United States. She later joined a multinational consulting company in Houston, TX, and helped several billion-dollar revenue energy companies on strategic planning. She then works for a fortune 500 corporation, providing business intelligence analysis.

Ms. Joy’s teaching experience:

Upon graduating from high school, Ms. Joy formed her own tutoring school. In addition to teaching high school math classes, Ms. Joy also managed more than 10 high school graduates to prepare junior students for the college entrance exam.

Ms. Joy was a math tutor to college students when she studied in Massachusetts, US. Ms. Joy not only has a great passion to help her student to achieve academic goals but also excelled at making the class interesting! She was very patient and highly skilled in explaining complex math problems in simple ways.

Ms. Joy was born in a family of educators.

Ms. Joy’s grandfather was HR for the Department of Education. He recruited and placed thousands of teachers in his 30-year career. Ms. Joy’s grandma was one of the high-performed STEM students in her village. She went to a teacher’s preparation school in the 1950s and became a geography teacher.

Ms. Joy’s mom was a math/mandarin/physics teacher in both elementary and junior high school. She also spent tremendous time training new teachers. Her classes were selected, recorded, and broadcasted citywide as the excellent class of the year to other teachers. She won more than 100 awards in her career including “best teacher of the year” and “outstanding educator of the year”!

Ms. Joy has learned multiple skills including teaching, recruiting qualified teachers, and most importantly motivating students to learn!

Besides the education world, Ms. Joy is passionate about helping other women to achieve academic and career goals by providing professional attire and career advice. She regularly donates to Dress for Success Houston, Goodwill donations, and United Way of Greater Houston.

Thank you for reading my story! I look forward to working with you and your students soon!
